Home page

Our Mission The goal of Ata Concrete is to provide reliable and efficient results for your home projects. About us Ata Concrete is a local company run by Rich and his reliable staff of workers. We are based in San Leandro, Ca and cover the entire East Bay region, also serving surrounding areas. We are well connected with local suppliers bringing you the best rates for your buck. We have reliable crews that can comfortably spread out to 3-4 projects if needed.

Company Information

Meet the owner Rich grew up in the bay area going to local schools in Oakland. While his friends were spending their summers camping and traveling, Rich stayed behind and helped his dad and uncles construct concrete projects. He grew a liking for the craft and willingly welcomed the challenges and problem-solving the work presented. He learned the value of hard work and sacrifice. When asked why concrete? His answer is, “I like taking people’s visions and bringing it to life, step by step.”
